About the Company

Trades that work together, under one team.

Halaza Enterprise (Pty) Ltd is a South African company providing high-quality construction, solar energy solutions, gas installations, electric fencing, maintenance and related engineering services.

We're built around one idea: a client shouldn't need five different contractors for one property. From foundations to fencing to the final electrical sign-off, we plan and deliver it as one job — safe, compliant and built to spec.
